# Telegram Bingo Game - Rules & How to Play

## Game Overview

Telegram Bingo is a real-time multiplayer bingo game where players mark numbers on their card as they are called. The first player to complete a winning pattern wins the prize pool.

## How to Play

### 1. Start Game
- Open the game from the Telegram menu
- Select your game mode (Demo, 10, 20, 50, or 100 coins)
- Your bingo card is automatically generated with 25 random numbers

### 2. Countdown
- Waiting room shows a countdown timer (usually 30 seconds)
- Other players join during this time
- Once countdown reaches 0, the game starts

### 3. Countdown Display
The countdown shows: **"Countdown tick: 24s remaining (elapsed: 5s)"**
- This shows how many seconds until the game begins
- Elapsed time tracks how long you've been waiting

### 4. Bingo Card
Your card has a 5x5 grid:
- **B column** - Numbers 1-15
- **I column** - Numbers 16-30
- **N column** - Numbers 31-45 (center is FREE)
- **G column** - Numbers 46-60
- **O column** - Numbers 61-75

The **center square is automatically FREE** (marked for you).

### 5. Number Calling
- Numbers are drawn randomly every 3 seconds
- Called numbers appear in the center of the screen
- Your card is **auto-marked** if Auto-Play is enabled
- If Auto-Play is off, tap numbers to mark them manually

### 6. Winning Patterns

Mark any of these patterns to win:

| Pattern | Description | Payout |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Horizontal Line** | Any complete row | 1x multiplier |
| **Vertical Line** | Any complete column | 1x multiplier |
| **Diagonal** | Corner to corner | 1.2x multiplier |
| **Four Corners** | All 4 corner squares | 1.5x multiplier |
| **Full House** | All 25 squares marked | 2x multiplier |

### 7. Claiming Win
- **Auto-detection** - Win is automatically detected when pattern completed
- **Manual claim** - Tap "CLAIM BINGO" button to claim your win
- First valid claim wins the entire prize pool

### 8. Prize Distribution
- **Winner gets** - 80% of total pool collected from all players
- **House gets** - 20% platform fee
- **Example** - 5 players stake 20 coins each = 100 coin pool → Winner gets 80 coins

## Settings & Controls

### Settings Menu (3-dot icon)

- **Set Stake: 10 coins** - Play in 10-coin mode
- **Set Stake: 20 coins** - Play in 20-coin mode
- Changing stake will adjust your actual game cost and potential winnings

#### Sound

- **Sound: Off** - Mute the sounds

### Game Controls

**Auto Play Toggle**
- **ON** - Numbers automatically marked when called
- **OFF** - You manually tap numbers to mark them

## Balance & Wallet

### Balance System
- **Coins** - Your main gaming currency
- **Bonus** - Extra free coins (some limited uses)
- View your balance at top of screen

### How Costs Work
1. You place a bet (select your stake: 10, 20, 50, or 100 coins)
2. Game starts, coins are reserved
3. If you **win** - Get stake back + winnings
4. If you **lose** - Coins are deducted from balance
5. Balance updates in real-time after game ends

### Insufficient Balance
- Warning appears if you don't have enough coins for selected stake
- Top up your wallet before playing
- Can set lower stake to play with available balance

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does a game take?
**A:** Usually 2-5 minutes from countdown start to winner declaration, depending on how quickly patterns complete.

### Q: What if two players win at the same time?
**A:** First claim to reach the server wins. Millisecond precision.

### Q: Can I play demo mode indefinitely?
**A:** Yes, demo mode is unlimited practice with no real coins involved.

### Q: Why didn't my mark register?
**A:** The number may have been called after you tapped. Refresh the page and try again.

### Q: What happens if I disconnect mid-game?
**A:** You're still in the game. Your auto-marks continue if enabled. Reconnect to see current status.

### Q: Do I get coins back if I lose?
**A:** No, losing stakes are deducted from your balance. Only winning games return the stake plus prize.

### Q: Why is the countdown frozen?
**A:** If countdown appears stuck, refresh the page. The server continues counting down even if display freezes.

### Q: Can I change my stake during a game?
**A:** No, stake is locked when you join. Change stake before starting a new game.

## Connection & Sync

### Countdown Synchronization
- Server sends countdown update every second
- If connection lags, frontend shows best estimate
- Display format: **"Remaining: 24s (Elapsed: 5s)"**

### Auto-Reconnection
- If connection drops, app automatically reconnects
- You stay in your game during reconnection
- All game state resumes from where you left off

### Server Connection Status
- Online indicator shown in game stats
- Green light = Connected
- Red light = Reconnecting
